Who We Are

A Trusted Advisor for Global Growth

Omnipoint LTD is a market research firm that helps its clients identify the right company and service at the right time. We advise clients on the market presence and service functionality of the companies we survey across industry sectors — so our clients can focus on what matters most: running their business.

We work with clients across multiple verticals to assess and implement the most effective strategies for scaling through technology partnerships and global logistics resources. As many of our clients are regional and global enterprises, they rely on us to identify the right partners for immediate impact while positioning their organizations for sustained long-term growth.

Our Approach

Rigorous Research. Confident Decisions.

Omnipoint LTD specializes in identifying and reviewing business-optimization interfaces and trusted third-party vendors to support global business continuity efforts. Our initial market research and rigorous partner vetting process are core to our value proposition — clients depend on our due diligence to streamline decision-making and avoid unnecessary time expenditure.
